## Cat Ratings (different from dogs)

1. **Affection Level** (1-5) - How cuddly/lap cat
2. **Activity Level** (1-5) - Energy/playfulness  
3. **Grooming Needs** (1-5) - Coat maintenance
4. **Vocality** (1-5) - How talkative
5. **Independence** (1-5) - Okay being alone
6. **Kid Friendly** (1-5)
7. **Dog Friendly** (1-5)
8. **Intelligence** (1-5)

## Size Categories
- Small: < 4 kg (8 lbs)
- Medium: 4-6 kg (8-13 lbs)
- Large: 6-9 kg (13-20 lbs)
- Extra Large: > 9 kg (Maine Coon, Savannah)
