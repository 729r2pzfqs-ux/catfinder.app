Persian vs Ragdoll

Two of the most beloved fluffy lap cats compared. Both are gentle and affectionate, but they have key differences in grooming, activity, and personality.

Persian

The Persian is one of the oldest and most popular cat breeds, known for its long, luxurious coat and sweet, gentle personality.

Calm Gentle Affectionate Quiet

Ragdoll

Ragdolls are known for going limp when picked up, hence their name. They're extremely docile, loving cats that follow their owners around.
